Our qualities

Mission

We are fully invested in ensuring that your needs are reliably met with diligence, professionalism, timeliness and a firm guarantee of quality. We prioritise efficiency, productivity and safety in our working environments as part of our commitment to providing world-class services. Thus, we constantly review and strive to improve everything we offer.

Top quality printing & packaging solutions

We understand how crucial it is for your brand, products and communication materials to stand out in the market place. That's why we offer full-fledged printing and packaging solutions with focus on quality and customization to take your business to the next level.

Vision

State of the art technology

We are the first in Malaysia to install Heidelberg and utilize the Speedmaster XL 105, with innovative perfecting technology to meet the most stringent demands in quality and productivity. We further augment our services with GPS tracking of deliveries and 80 CCTV units on-premise.

From 1963 to the 21st century

We are one of the earliest offset printing companies in Malaysia. In 1998, we achieved the ISO9001 ISO Quality Management Systems and will set our sights on the industry benchmark in the future with the ISO12657-2 print standard.
