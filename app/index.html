Our
journey

1959

Our founder went overseas to study printing in Japan on photographic masking, color separation, color restoring, screening and plate making at Toppan Printing Company, Haboshi Plant.

1962

Graduated from Toppan Printing Company.

1963

Graphic Press was established in a humble scale in a shoplot at Brickfields. Started with Commercial Printing.

1964

Entry to Security Printing, Commissioned the Social & Welfare Services Lottery ticket.

1967

Expand and moved to a factory premise at Jalan 225 (Petaling Jaya)

1984

Entry to Label Printing, more than a billion pieces of labels printed as of today.

1995

Expand and moved to larger factory in PJS3/2 (Petaling Jaya).

1997

Entry to Packaging Printing.

1998

Joint-Venture with Toppan Forms (S) Pte Ltd.

2004

Entry to Beer Label Printing.

2005

Heidelberg Most Prestigious User Award 2005.

2010

Entry to Packing Service.

2011

ISO9001:2008 certification

2013

50 years of printing expertise at your service.

2015

ISO12647-2:2013 certification

Entry to UV Printing.

2017

Our Managing Director, Mr. Tony Wong, was bestowed the prestigious Malaysia Print Industry Lifetime Achievement Award at the Malaysia Print Awards 2017 by SFTCPPA.

Some of Graphic Press Group's key milestones since its establishment in 1963.

Our qualities

Mission

We are fully invested in ensuring that your needs are reliably met with diligence, professionalism, timeliness and a firm guarantee of quality. We prioritise efficiency, productivity and safety in our working environments as part of our commitment to providing world-class services. Thus, we constantly review and strive to improve everything we offer.

Top quality printing & packaging solutions

We understand how crucial it is for your brand, products and communication materials to stand out in the market place. This is why we offer full-fledged offset printing and packaging solutions with focus on quality and customization to take your business to the next level.

Vision

State of the art technology

We are the first in Malaysia to install Heidelberg Speedmaster XL 105 and Heidelberg SX 74 Hybrid UV, combining innovative Heidelberg Pressroom Manager and Image Controller technologies to meet the most stringent demands in quality and productivity. We further augment our services with GPS tracking on our deliveries and have installed more than 160 CCTV units on the premise.

From 1963 to the 21st century

We are one of the earliest offset printing companies in Malaysia. In 1998, we achieved the ISO9001-2008 Quality Management Systems and recently upgraded to ISO9001-2015 standard. In 2015, we achieved the ISO12647-2:2013 International Standard Certification for Print Color Management.
